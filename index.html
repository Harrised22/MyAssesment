This is a fun tribute page to our loyal companions!

ISIS

June 1, 2001 - April 11, 2011

Isis was a beautiful Rottwieler mixed with German Shephard. She was like the daughter I never had and I miss her dearly.

  1. Isis would wait until I fell asleep and climb into my bed.
  2. Isis would bring me her favorite ball when she wanted to play.
  3. Isis would stand by the door when she needed to go outside.
